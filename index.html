Who You'll Hear From

SE

Sarah R. Enterline

Author · Apologist · Women's Ministry Leader

Sarah is an author, apologist, pastor's wife, mother, teacher, and women's ministry leader. She holds undergraduate degrees in Biblical Studies, Christian Studies (Theology & Ministry), Social and Behavioral Science, and Philosophy, along with a California State Credential in History and an M.A. in Christian Leadership. Sarah speaks at churches and conferences around the country and has guested on numerous radio shows and podcasts.
